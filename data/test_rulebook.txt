# Adventure Quest - Board Game Rules

## Game Overview
Adventure Quest is an exciting board game for 2-4 players, ages 10 and up. Embark on a thrilling journey through mystical lands, battle fierce monsters, and collect powerful artifacts to become the ultimate adventurer!

## Components
- 1 Game Board
- 4 Player Tokens
- 60 Adventure Cards
- 30 Monster Cards
- 20 Treasure Tokens
- 4 Character Sheets
- 2 Six-Sided Dice

## Objective
Be the first player to collect 5 Treasure Tokens and return to the Village Center.

## Setup
1. Place the game board in the center of the table.
2. Each player chooses a character token and places it on the Village Center space.
3. Shuffle the Adventure and Monster cards separately and place them face-down.
4. Place Treasure Tokens in a pile near the board.

## Game Flow
### On Your Turn:
1. Roll both dice and move your token that many spaces.
2. Follow the instructions for the space you land on:
   - **Forest**: Draw an Adventure Card
   - **Mountain**: Draw a Monster Card
   - **Treasure Chest**: Collect 1 Treasure Token
   - **Village Center**: If you have 5 Treasure Tokens, you win!

### Adventure Cards
These cards present challenges, puzzles, or opportunities. Some require skill checks using dice rolls.

### Monster Cards
When you draw a Monster Card, you must defeat the monster through combat. Roll dice to determine success.

## Combat Rules
1. The player rolls 2 dice.
2. Add your character's strength bonus.
3. If the total equals or exceeds the monster's difficulty, you win.
4. If you lose, you lose 1 Treasure Token.

## Winning the Game
The first player to collect 5 Treasure Tokens and return to the Village Center wins the game!

## Advanced Rules
For experienced players, add the following rules:
- **Magic Spells**: Players can cast spells once per game
- **Allies**: Players can form temporary alliances
- **Traps**: Some spaces trigger trap cards

Enjoy your Adventure Quest!
